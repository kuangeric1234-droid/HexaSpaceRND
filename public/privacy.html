Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026 · Hexa Space Pty Ltd · 402/830 Whitehorse Road, Box Hill VIC 3128, Australia

Hexa Space Pty Ltd (“Hexa Space”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your personal information when you use our website (hexaspace.com.au), our member portal (portal.hexaspace.com.au) and our member mobile app (together, the “Services”).

We handle personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). By using the Services you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

We only collect personal information that is reasonably necessary to provide the Services.

Information you give us

  • Account & identity: your name, email address and the password you set to access the portal or app.
  • Business details: business/company name, contact person, ABN, industry and country (for members and prospective members).
  • Billing details: billing contact, invoices, and — where you set up direct debit or card payment — the details required to process payment (see Payments).
  • Bookings & orders: meeting-room, studio and event bookings, and food/drink orders you place through the app (items, delivery location, notes).
  • Mail & deliveries: records of mail and parcels received on your behalf at reception, which may include a photo of the item taken to help you identify it.
  • Enquiries & communications: messages, event registrations, tour and proposal requests, and correspondence you send us.

Information we collect automatically

  • Usage & device data: IP address, browser/device type, pages viewed and general interaction data, collected to operate and secure the Services.
  • Access & printing: where you use member facilities, records such as door-access events and print jobs (including a personal print PIN) associated with your account.
  • Cookies: see Cookies & analytics.

Information from others

We may receive information from the primary contact at your organisation (for example when they invite you as a member), from referral partners, and from our service providers listed below.

2. How we use your information

  • Provide, operate and administer memberships, leases/licences, bookings and the Services;
  • Process invoices and payments and manage your account;
  • Provision building services you use — door access, printing, mail handling and food/drink ordering;
  • Send transactional messages (e.g. invoices, order and mail notifications, booking confirmations, access details);
  • Respond to enquiries and provide support;
  • Improve, secure and troubleshoot the Services and prevent fraud or misuse;
  • Send you marketing about Hexa Space where permitted (you can opt out at any time — see Marketing); and
  • Meet our legal, accounting and record-keeping obligations.

4. Payments

Card and direct-debit payments are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your full card number on our systems — it is handled and stored by Stripe under the PCI-DSS standard. We retain limited details (such as the last four digits and a secure payment token) to identify and manage your payments. Stripe’s handling of your data is governed by the Stripe Privacy Policy.

5. Door access & mobile key

Door access at our premises is provided through Salto KS. When your membership is active we provide your name and email to Salto KS so a mobile key can be issued to you; you unlock doors using the Salto KS mobile app on your own device. Access and entry events may be logged for security and audit purposes. Salto KS processes this information under its own privacy terms.

6. Overseas disclosure

Some of our service providers store and process data on servers located outside Australia, including in the United States and the European Union. Where we disclose personal information overseas, we take reasonable steps to ensure it is handled consistently with the Australian Privacy Principles and applicable law.

7. How we protect your information

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss and unauthorised access, modification or disclosure — including encrypted transport (HTTPS), authenticated access, access controls, and reputable infrastructure providers. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; while we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. How long we keep it

We keep personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the Services and to meet our legal, tax and accounting obligations (for financial records, generally at least seven years). When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify it.

9. Cookies & analytics

Our website and portal use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and understand how the Services are used so we can improve them. We may use analytics and advertising measurement (such as Google) which set their own cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect how the Services work.

10. Marketing & your choices

We may send you information about Hexa Space, events and offers where permitted by law. Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link, and you can opt out at any time by contacting us at info@hexaspace.com.au. Opting out of marketing does not stop essential service messages (such as invoices, booking and access notifications).

11. Accessing & correcting your information

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, and ask us to correct it if it is inaccurate, out of date or incomplete. Contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable time and may need to verify your identity first. There is generally no charge, though we may recover reasonable costs for substantial access requests.

12. Children

The Services are intended for businesses and their team members and are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

13. Complaints

If you have a concern about how we have handled your personal information, please contact us first at info@hexaspace.com.au and we will work to resolve it. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au or 1300 363 992.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at this address and the “Last updated” date shows when it last changed. Material changes will be notified through the Services where appropriate.
